Software Engineer - Robotics

Are you a software enthusiast with a passion for robotics? Join our team of talented engineers to develop and optimize software solutions for our robotic systems.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with hardware engineers to integrate control algorithms into our robotic platforms.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Robotics, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, C++, and ROS.
  • Experience with robotic frameworks and simulation tools.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
